About Us

Welcome to Fluffy Paw, where we believe every dog deserves a loving home! Our dedicated team works tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs of all breeds and sizes. At Fluffy Paw, we prioritize the well-being of our furry friends and strive to match each dog with their perfect forever family. Join us in our mission to provide a safe and happy future for every pup.

Founded in 2010, Fluffy Paw has grown from a small group of passionate animal lovers into a thriving sanctuary for dogs in need. Over the past decade, we have successfully rehomed over 5,000 dogs, each finding their unique forever family. Our team, composed of over 50 dedicated employees and countless volunteers, works around the clock to ensure every dog receives the love, care, and attention they deserve.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses up to 200 dogs at any given time, providing spacious living areas, comfortable sleeping quarters, and a variety of enrichment activities to keep our furry residents happy and healthy. We offer comprehensive veterinary care, behavioral training, and personalized support to prepare our dogs for their new homes.

At Fluffy Paw, we believe in community and collaboration. We work closely with local shelters, rescue groups, and veterinarians to maximize our impact and reach as many dogs as possible. Through education and outreach programs, we aim to raise awareness about responsible pet ownership and the importance of adoption.

Join us at Fluffy Paw and be part of a community dedicated to giving every dog a chance at a loving, happy life. Whether you’re looking to adopt, volunteer, or support our mission in other ways, we welcome you with open arms. Together, we can make a difference—one wagging tail at a time.
